Full list of features

- Contains every Splatoon 1 map, weapon, sub and special weapons back in Splatoon 2.

- Planned custom splatfests.

- Two different versions of the mod, Gambit which has all the Splatoon 1 replacement stuff and Blitz version, which has Splatoon 2 Square, music and etc.

- and much more in the way!.
